About the system

Purpose and values of the Digital Patient Record Management System for community health in Sierra Leone.

Why this system exists

Community health centers in Sierra Leone need a simple, reliable way to keep patient records digital. This system supports consistent care, reduces paperwork, and helps staff find the right information quickly.

Our values

  • Patient-first: Every feature aims to support better, safer care.
  • Privacy: Patient data is protected with access controls and secure storage.
  • Usability: Staff can learn and use the system without lengthy training.

In the community

Supporting health centers and patients across Sierra Leone.
